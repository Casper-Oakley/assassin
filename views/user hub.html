Salford Rejex Team Page

The home of NUEL competitors, The Salford Rejex

Who we are

 The Salford Rejex is a competitive 5v5 League of Legends team, competing in the NUEL, the team consists of undergrads in varying stages of their degrees from their first to third years of tertiary education.

What it is

The NUEL (National University Esports League) is a round robin format tournament in which competitors play a variety of online games to compete to be the best university in the country at said game. The Salford Rejex plays in the 5v5 year long League of Legends tournament in an effort to compete to be the strongest LoL team in the country, or at least have fun trying so.

This page will keep track of the Rejex's progress and team structure.
